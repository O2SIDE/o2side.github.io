Mind Voyage

The passion project

Founded in 2020, Mind Voyage is O2SIDE's baby. Our goal is simple: Ask the most intriguing questions we can think of with the most interesting people we can find.

We've published 20 episodes in under a year and we continue to gain traction and publicity as we grow.
